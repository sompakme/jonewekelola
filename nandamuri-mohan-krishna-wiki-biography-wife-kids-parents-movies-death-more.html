Nandamuri Mohan Krishna Wiki, Biography, Wife, Kids, Parents, Movies, Death &amp; More

Nandamuri Mohan Krishna (born 2 September 1956; Age: 66 Years) is a well-known cinematographer, media face, politician, celebrity child, and internet personality from Hyderabad, Telangana, India. He is known in the country as the son of the famous South Indian actor and politician named Nandamuri Taraka Rama Rao, who popularly goes by the name N. T. Rama Rao.

As everyone knows that N. T. Rama Rao was the 10th Chief Minister of United Andhra Pradesh and served his position with complete grace and responsibility.

Who is Nandamuri Mohan Krishna, N. T. Rama Rao’s Son?

Besides being a celebrity child, Mohan Krishna has established his own name in the film industry. Well, he has not worked in front of the cameras but has a successful career behind the cameras. Reportedly, he is a cinematographer by profession and looks after the entire photography department. As per the sources, he debuts his profession in the film industry in 1983 with the ‘Chanda Sasanudu’ film which was a major hit.

Besides this, he has also worked on other projects such as Gharwali Baharwali, Ansuyammagari Alludu, Goppinti Alludu, Ramudu Bheemudu, and more. In addition to this, he is also a politician and has worked in Indian politics as well. His main objective is to follow in the footsteps of his dad and helps a lot of people.
