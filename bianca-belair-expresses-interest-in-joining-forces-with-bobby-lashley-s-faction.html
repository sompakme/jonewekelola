Bianca Belair Expresses Interest in Joining Forces with Bobby Lashleys Faction

Bianca Belair is the EST of WWE and has worked very hard to get where she is in the company. She has been a babyface for a long time and fans have been wondering if Belair will ever return to the dark side and join Bobby Lashley and The Street Profits. Interestingly enough, Belair has confirmed she is more than interested in joining the faction.

The former RAW Women’s Champion truly loves her husband Montez Ford and they have quickly established themselves as one of the power couples in WWE. That’s exactly why fans want them to work together properly.

In an Instagram Q&A session, Belair was asked if she would be interested in joining Lashley and the Street Profits. The former SmackDown Women’s Champion wasted no time in confirming she would love to do that.

 “It would be dope.”

Bobby Lashley and the Street Profits have been working together for the past several months but their booking has largely been inconsistent due to the stop-start nature of their booking.

Bianca Belair is currently teaming up with Shotzi as they continue to feud with Damage CTRL. Only time will tell whether the EST of WWE will end up joining Lashley’s faction this year.
