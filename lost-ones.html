Lost Ones

Bump Lost Ones, the latest cut from J. Cole. It was released on Monday, June 13th, 2011. Things are improving for J. Cole with each new offering, and Lost Ones is certainly no exception. It's a worthy addition to the J. Cole's catalogue, which has seen much evolution as of late. We're definitely anticipating the next step.

Have you heard J. Cole's Work Out? If you're down, make sure to visit J. Cole's profile page for the newest info, news, songs, and mixtapes.
