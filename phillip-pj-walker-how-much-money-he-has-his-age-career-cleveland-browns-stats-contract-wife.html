Phillip 'PJ' Walker: How Much Money He Has, His Age, Career, Cleveland Browns, Stats, Contract, Wife

Post Last Updates by Ankit: Friday, March 1, 2024 @ 8:14 PM

News: As per NFL Network’s Ian Rapoport’s report, PJ Walker is poised to assume the role of the Cleveland Browns’ starting quarterback for the remainder of the season. This choice has been made due to Deshaun Watson’s absence, resulting from a shoulder injury that necessitated surgery.

Watson to Undergo Surgery and Be Placed on Injured Reserve

Rapoport has confirmed that Watson will undergo surgery and subsequently be placed on injured reserve (IR). In light of this development, Walker will step in as the team’s primary quarterback, with Dorian Thompson-Robinson taking on the role of his backup.

Walker’s Personal Life and Financial Status

As Walker assumes the starting quarterback position, there has been a surge in curiosity about his personal life and financial situation. At present, Walker’s estimated net worth stands at $1 million, primarily sourced from his earnings with the Carolina Panthers. His current contract, a three-year agreement valued at $8.5 million, is set to yield a base salary of $1.3 million for the year 2023.

A Successful Career in College and Professional Football

Born on February 26, 1995, PJ Walker is presently 28 years old and serves as the quarterback for the Cleveland Browns in the NFL. During his college tenure at Temple University, Walker distinguished himself in several aspects, such as passing yards, touchdown passes, and victories achieved as a starting quarterback.

Walker’s NFL and XFL Career

In addition to his tenure with the Browns, Walker has also showcased his skills while playing for the Carolina Panthers and the Houston Roughnecks in the XFL. His professional journey began when he signed with the Indianapolis Colts as an undrafted free agent in 2017.

Throughout his NFL career, Walker has participated in 20 games, including nine starts. Over this period, he has maintained a completion rate of 54.9%, accumulating 2,079 passing yards, 6 touchdowns, and 16 interceptions. Additionally, he has contributed 81 rushing yards to his team’s efforts. Notably, Walker’s standout season was in 2022, when he started in five games, amassing 731 passing yards, 3 touchdowns, and 3 interceptions. As of now, his career passer rating stands at 60.1.

New Contract with the Cleveland Browns

In 2023, Walker secured a two-year contract with the Cleveland Browns valued at $4 million. This contract encompasses a $1 million signing bonus and a guaranteed sum of $2.01 million. His base salary for the upcoming season stands at $1.01 million, translating to a cap hit of $617,222.

Off the Field: Personal Life

Off the field, PJ Walker is happily married to his longtime girlfriend, Ashlei.
