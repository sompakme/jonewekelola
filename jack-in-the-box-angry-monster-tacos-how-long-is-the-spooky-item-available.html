Jack in the Box Angry Monster Tacos: How long is the spooky item available?

IN THE spirit of spooky season, Jack in the Box has unveiled new Halloween-themed menu items.

Now, customers want to know how to purchase the limited-edition items from the popular fast-food chain.

How long are the Angry Monster Tacos available?

Jack in the Box introduced the Angry Monster Taco as part of its Halloween 2023 menu.

According to the company's press release, the new item is a spicy version of the Monster Taco, which returned to the spooky menu for the third year in a row.

Both tacos are priced at two for $3 and are available from September 25 to November 19, 2023.

The press release states: "Jack in the Box champions tacos of all sizes – from tiny tacos to monster-sized and knows variety matters.

"An order of Monster Tacos includes two monster-sized tacos with melted American cheese, shredded lettuce, and taco sauce. Its spicy counterpart, Angry Monster Tacos, are a spicy alternative wrapped in a red shell."

What other items are available on Jack in the Box's Halloween Menu?

Multiple other items are also available on Jack in the Box's Halloween Menu, including the Basic Witch Shake and Pumpkin Croissant Bites.

Like the Monster Taco and Angry Monster Taco, these items became available for purchase on September 25 and will remain on the menu until November 19, 2023.

Per ABC 4 Utah, the Basic Witch Shake contains "Jack’s Pumpkin Spice Oreo Cookie Shake made with Oreo Cookie pieces, along with a whipped topping and a cherry."

The Pumpkin Croissant Bites are "bite-sized, buttery, flaky croissants stuffed with a warm, sweet pumpkin filling."

What is Jack in the Box's favorite season?

Jack in the Box's favorite season appears to be autumn, as according to the press release, Halloween is his "favorite night of the year."

According to ABC 4 Utah, another release states: "Jack Box is famously known as the King of Late Nights, so naturally Halloween is his favorite holiday."
