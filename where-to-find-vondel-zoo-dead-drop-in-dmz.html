Where To Find Vondel Zoo Dead Drop In DMZ

Here's the exact location of the Vondel Zoo Dead Drop in Warzone 2's DMZ mode.

First Published June 29, 2023, 10:19

Since the release of Warzone 2 in November 2022, the game has continued to grow exponentially, adding new features, maps, and interactive quests. With Season 4 having dropped in June, we have seen a flood of new content, including the new Warzone and DMZ map Vondel for players to battle and explore. Unlike the vast barren wastelands of Al Mazrah, Vondel is a mid-sized map based inside urban territory, resembling the original Warzone downtown district in map Verdansk.

Players are currently enjoying the new map on game mode DMZ, battling the powers that be and smashing quests and tasks bestowed upon them. With that said Warzone players have been looking all over DMZ maps for certain Dead Drops situated throughout Al Mazrah, Ashika Island, and Vondel. Within this guide, we're going to show you where to find the Vondel Zoo Dead Drop!

Where Is The Vondel Zoo Dead Drop In DMZ?

The exact location of the Dead Drop located in sector D7 in Vondel Zoo can be seen above, specifically on the south side of the building inside of a rubbish bin. Please act with caution around these areas because all around the marked building, west road, and on the Dead Drop are enemy AI spawns, therefore you're going to want to keep an eye out!

The drop is located on the West side of the zoo, therefore players will not have to venture too far into the location of interest, making for an easier escape. Don't let this dull your senses — players will still find themselves in trouble running into enemy AI within and outside the compound. 

Players may want to equip a thermal scope when attempting to find this Dead Drop, this is due to the open space the player will find themselves in, especially with the fog that smothers the surface of the map. The thermal scope will help you swiftly take out any enemies around the Dead Drop, additionally aiding you in your ability to quickly obtain and escape with the contents of the Dead Drop.
