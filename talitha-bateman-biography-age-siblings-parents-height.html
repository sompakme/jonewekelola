Talitha Bateman Biography, Age, Siblings, Parents, Height

Fans of the 2017 horror movie; Annabelle: Creation and the 2018 romance comedy Love, Simon are no strangers to Talitha Bateman’s talent. This actress who started her career as a child has now become a household name in the film industry, from the roles she continues to land to the stellar performances she brings to every movie, she is definitely a star to watch. To learn more about the life of Talitha Bateman, keep reading.

Talitha Bateman Biography (Age)

Bateman was born on September 4, 2001 in Turlock, California. She has no formal education and does not plan to get one in the future, this includes getting a degree.

When Talitha was still a two-year-old, her parents could tell she had caught the acting bug. They acted accordingly and decided to support his decision. The exact year is unknown, but her decision to move to Los Angeles with her sister is what shaped her career. Her acting career began in 2012, when she was 11 years old.

The first role she got was in 2013 on the television show, The Middle which chronicled the experiences of a middle-class American family. The role was small and from there his career grew. She then had roles on the Agence Maker Shack, but the show was not picked up after its pilot episode aired.

After her first two appearances on these TV shows, her next performance is the one she is most remembered for, it was on the 2015 comedy TV show Hart of Dixie. The same year, she was cast in the science fiction films The Hive and The Fifth Wave, where she played the roles of Kayla and Teacup respectively.

Talitha Bateman’s career has come a long way since her unnoticed role in The Middle. Other movies and TV shows where she starred So B. It (2016), Revenge: A Love Story (2017), Stuck in the Middle (2017), and Geostorm (2017).

She also lent her acting skills to the animated film. The Boxcar Children: Surprise Island (2018) and specifically in Annabelle: Creation. Bateman explained during his performance in Annabelle that a lot of improvisation had to be done and shooting the movie was a lot scarier than what audiences saw.

With a career that started at a very young age, Talitha Bateman delivering impressive performances on the big screen is not to be missed. The awards and nominations haven’t arrived yet, but that hasn’t stopped this young woman who has never attended an acting class from becoming the star she already is.

Parents and Siblings

Talitha Bateman is the seventh child of her parents; Jonelle and Tim Bateman, who had seven more children. Bateman grew up in what can best be described as an unconventional household where she and her seven siblings – Gabriel, Noah, Judah, Justin, Aleq, Hannah and Leah were all homeschooled.

One of their family’s traditions, as Talitha Bateman revealed in an interview, all children received a rose on their birthday. Their parents started this tradition when their first child was still a child and it continues. They hope that each time each of the eight gets married, their spouse will carry on the tradition.
