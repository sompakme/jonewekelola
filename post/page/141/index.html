Rizzoli &amp; Isles to End After Season Seven

published on September 03, 2024
Rizzoli & Isles is getting ready to say goodbye. The upcoming seventh season of the TNT drama will be its last, network president Kevin Reilly announced Thursday at the Television Critics Association’s winter press tour. The show, which ranks as the Turner-owned cable network’s second-most-watched series ever, behind only The Closer, will return in the summer for an abbreviated 13-episode season.  Reilly told reporters that stars Sasha Alexander and Angie Harmon have “had really satisfying runs,” and that he wasn’t aware whether or not the duo would or wouldn’t do more of the series.
