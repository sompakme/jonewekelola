Rikishi Net Worth

Rikishi, born Solofa Fatu, is a Samoan professional wrestler best known for his time with the WWE. He was born on October 11, 1965 in San Francisco, CA and is remembered for his high-flying moves and colorful personality. He is a two-time Intercontinental Champion and a one-time World Tag Team Champion.
