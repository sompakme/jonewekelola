40 funniest MLB The Show names to try out before 2023 release

MLB The Show 23 is all set to hit the market on March 28, while some may have cashed in on the early access opportunity already. The iconic baseball video game has already gotten fans excited with its newest additions.

MLB The Show was created by San Diego Studios, and fans can enjoy the game on PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox Series S/X, Xbox One, Nintendo Switch, and PC.

New features have also been added for the Diamond Dynasty, the most popular game mode across MLB The Show's history. This includes the option to acquire 99-rated players from day one itself, as well as the introduction of Sets and Seasons.
