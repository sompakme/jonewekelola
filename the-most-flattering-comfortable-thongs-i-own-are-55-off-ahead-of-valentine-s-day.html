The secret is the brand’s V-front and -back waistband — its signature design feature — which slopes and falls in just the right places. Equal parts flattering and functional, the band holds the thong snugly against the skin, making them impervious to slipping or bunching. And though they are secure, these thongs are anything but tight; the waistband is crafted from stretchy, lay-flat lace that resists digging into the sides of the skin. Moreover, the bunch-proof band makes for traceless wear under any garment (read: no VPL). 

The band is made from a nylon and spandex blend, which defies the scratchy feel lace-trimmed underwear can often impart. As for the rest of the thong, the body is 100 percent nylon, and the interior lining is made from high-quality, breathable cotton. The overall fabric composition makes these thongs comfortable and durable. In fact, the five-pack I snagged last summer is holding up pristinely. The colors and fabric look as vibrant and new as the day I, er, decided to nab them from the bachelorette gift bags. 

Frankly — and, again, selfishly — I’m reluctant to even spread news of this stellar sale for fear of spurring a sold-out status before I can snag ‘em for myself. But, if you’re considering shopping the Hanky Panky thong sale, I’d advise doing so this very instant. I’m not alone in my fervor for these thongs, so act quickly if you want a pack for yourself before they’re gone; when fan-favorites are over 50 percent off, it’s only a matter of time before they’re sold out — especially with Valentine’s Day right around the corner.
