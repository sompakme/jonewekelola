James McAvoy Net Worth

James McAvoy is a Scottish actor born on April 21, 1979 in Glasgow, Scotland. He is best known for his roles in Atonement, The Last King of Scotland, and the X-Men series. He has also had roles in Frank Herbert's Children of Dune and Shameless on television. McAvoy has become a prominent figure in the entertainment industry.

About

Scottish actor who rose to prominence for his roles in Atonement, The Last King of Scotland and the X-Men series. In his television career, he has played roles in Frank Herbert's Children of Dune and Shameless. 

Before Fame

He made his acting debut in the 1995 film The Near Room at the age of 15. He continued adding to his portfolio with television roles, and appeared in Bollywood Queen in 2003. 

Trivia

He jumped off the roof of the world's tallest hospital to raise awareness and money for a Ugandan children's charity. 

Family Life

His mother sent him to live with his grandparents when he was seven. He married Actress and co-star Anne-Marie Duff in 2006 and the couple split in 2016. He and Anne-Marie have a son named Brendan.

Associated With

He was the lead actor and love interest of Angelina Jolie in the blockbuster hit Wanted.
