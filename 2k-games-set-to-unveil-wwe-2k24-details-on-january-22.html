2K Games Set to Unveil WWE 2K24 Details on January 22

2K Games, the publisher of the WWE 2K series, has teased some news regarding WWE 2K24.

The official WWE Games Twitter account changed its profile picture to a WWE 2K24 logo and updated its bio to read, “More to come 1.22.24 — #WWE2K24.”

This suggests that there will be a major announcement or reveal related to WWE 2K24 on January 22, 2024. Fans of the WWE 2K video game series can look forward to learning more about the upcoming installment and any new features or improvements that may be introduced.

It’s worth noting that WWE 2K23, the previous installment in the series, featured John Cena as the cover athlete and was released on March 14, 2023.

As the release date for WWE 2K24 is not yet confirmed, fans will have to stay tuned to Ringside News for further updates from 2K Games.
