Video of T.I. cheating on Tiny Watch the clip discussed on T.I. &amp; Tiny: Friends &amp; Family H

T.I and Tiny are returning to VH1 with a brand-new series. In the T.I. & Tiny: Friends & Family Hustle premiere episode, the T.I. addresses a video in which it appears that he was caught cheating.

T.I claims in a clip on the series that he is “flabbergasted” by the rumor; however, it doesn’t seem like Tiny is satisfied with that answer.

The infamous video was published on The Shade Room earlier this year in June. The Atlanta rapper appears to be backstage after a concert in Indiana in the secretly recorded video.

In the recording, T.I appears to be getting cozy with an unidentified woman, who is not Tiny Harris. 

T.I was rumored to be in a relationship with Instagram model Bernice Burgos. However, multiple reports claim that T.I was caught cheating with singer Asia’h Epperson in the viral video. 

T.I blames social media for Tiny’s reaction to the video and said the situation would have been resolved if it was handled privately between the couple. 

T.I. & Tiny: Friends & Family Hustle

The 38-year-old, who has been married to Tiny since 2010 stated that other people’s opinions on the video are affecting her judgment. 

On the premiere episode of VH1’s T.I. & Tiny: The Family Hustle, the Live Your Life rapper explains the following in the confessional:

“The video ain’t really the issue. The video is posted up on social media is the issue. If someone had sent her that video privately, she and I would’ve had one conversation about it, and it would’ve been over.” 

One of the bombshells in T.I. & Tiny: Friends & Family Hustle is that Letoya Luckett used to date T.I. 

T.I. & Tiny: Friends & Family Hustle premieres this Monday, October 22 at 9/8c on VH1.
