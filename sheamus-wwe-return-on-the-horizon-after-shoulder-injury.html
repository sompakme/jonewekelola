Sheamus WWE Return on the Horizon After Shoulder Injury

Sheamus had a great career in WWE, winning multiple championships during his time in the company. The Celtic Warrior has been away for a while due to injury but WWE is already planning his return.

It was previously reported that Sheamus is out of action with a bad shoulder injury. The timing of his hiatus is notable because his WWE contract will be expiring next year – which is another topic of discussion entirely.

Last month, Ridge Holland walked out on Butch during tag team match on Smackdown, making it clear something interesting is in the works. While speaking on Wrestling Observer Radio, Dave Meltzer talked about Sheamus’ return from injury. He noted that The Celtic Warrior is “supposed to be back real soon.”

It is evident that Sheamus will return and come to help Butch instead of Ridge Holland. We will have to wait and see when The Celtic Warrior will make his triumphant return to WWE television.
