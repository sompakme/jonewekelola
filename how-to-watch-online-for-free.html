How To Watch Online for Free

After four feature films and another one on its way for the big screen, Legendary Pictures’ MonsterVerse is coming to the small screen with Monarch: Legacy of Monsters.

Debuting Friday on Apple TV+, the new series is set a year after the G-Day attack in 2014’s Godzilla. It follows Cate (Anna Sawai), a Bay Area school teacher who goes to Tokyo to learn more about her deceased father. She soon discovers that she has a secret half-brother (Ren Watabe), while her family has ties to a top-secret organization that researches the world’s monsters called Monarch.

Where to Watch Other MonsterVerse Movies

Monarch: Legacy of Monsters is the fifth installment in the MonsterVerse cinematic universe with King Kong and Godzilla. The franchise started in 2014 with the Gareth Edwards-directed Godzilla, which was followed up with Kong: Skull Island in 2017 and Godzilla: King of the Monsters in 2019. The two monsters battled each other in Godzilla vs. Kong in 2021, while the duo is expected to team up in Godzilla x Kong: The New Empire in early 2024.
