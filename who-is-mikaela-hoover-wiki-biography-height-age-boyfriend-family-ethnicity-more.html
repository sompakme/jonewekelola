Who is Mikaela Hoover? Wiki, Biography, Height, Age, Boyfriend, Family, Ethnicity &amp; More

Mikaela Hoover (born July 12, 1984; Age: 37 years) is a well-known actress, entrepreneur, model, social media star, television personality, and media face from Washington, United States. She became popular in the country after acted as Madison Westerbrook in WB’s Sorority Forever TV series. 

She has also featured in other TV series and movies such as Lost Dream, Fleshlightning, Another Happy Anniversary, Back in the Day, Cuz-Bros, Fantasy Life, Alexander IRL, Guest House, and many more. 

Apart from this, she also runs her own Makeup brand under the name ‘Moonlit’. She provides a wide range of lipsticks and mascara for the ladies. All the products are of their best quality and affordable prices. Currently, she is working on her upcoming projects like The Suicide Squad, Daddy’s Home, and Love Hard.

Mikaela Hoover’s Biography

Mikaela took birth on 12 July 1984 in Colbert, Washington, United States. Her full birth name is Mikaela Mehrizi Hoover. ‘Mehrizi’ is her mother’s surname and ‘Hoover’ is the surname of her dad. From her Facebook account, we found that her mother fondly calls her ‘Munch’. 

She took admission to a local school for her basic studies and high school graduation. Later, she enrolled herself in Los Angeles’ Loyola Marymount University for her bachelor’s degree in Theatre. She was interested in dancing and acting since childhood.
