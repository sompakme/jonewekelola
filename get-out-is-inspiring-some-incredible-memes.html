Get Out Is Inspiring Some Incredible Memes

Jordan Peele (of Key & Peele)’s directorial debut Get Out released on Friday, and already has blown expectations out of the water. Between making over $35 million in the opening weekend to inspiring Chance The Rapper to buy out an entire theater and give away tickets to the movie for free, the film is clearly something special.

Not everyone loved the film, with some people complaining about the film’s "racist" undertones. However, those people a very loud but small minority, as the film has positive ratings pretty much everywhere you look.
