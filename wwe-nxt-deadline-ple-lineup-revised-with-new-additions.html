WWE NXT Deadline PLE Lineup Revised with New Additions

Get ready for an action-packed night of wrestling showdowns at the upcoming WWE NXT Deadline PLE event in Bridgeport, CT, on December 9th. The updated lineup promises some epic battles that you won’t want to miss:

Main Event: WWE NXT Championship Match Ilja Dragunov, the reigning champion, is set to defend his title against none other than the ruthless Baron Corbin. Brace yourself for an intense clash as these two superstars vie for supremacy.

Men’s Iron Survivor Challenge: It’s an all-out war in the Men’s Iron Survivor Challenge, featuring a lineup of incredible talent:

  • Dijak
  • Trick Williams
  • Josh Briggs
  • Two more competitors who are yet to be unveiled. The suspense is killing us!

Women’s Iron Survivor Challenge: The Women’s Iron Survivor Challenge promises to be a fierce battle with these fierce competitors:

  • Tiffany Stratton
  • Lash Legend
  • Blair Davenport
  • Plus, two more competitors still shrouded in mystery. Who will emerge as the ultimate survivor?

The WWE NXT Deadline PPV is shaping up to be a night of unforgettable moments and non-stop action. Mark your calendars for December 9th, as Bridgeport, CT, becomes the epicenter of the wrestling world. Don’t miss out on the excitement!

As a reminder, Ringside News will have live play-by-play coverage of the event.
