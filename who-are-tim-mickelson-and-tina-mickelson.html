Who are Tim Mickelson and Tina Mickelson?

Phil Mickelson was born Philip Alfred Mickelson.

Phil Mickelson is a professional golfer who currently plays in the LIV Golf League.

Phil Mickelson has won 45 events on the PGA Tour including six major championships: three Masters titles, two PGA Championships, and one Open Championship.

Phil Mickelson was born on June 16, 1970. He was born in San Diego, California, United States.

Phil Mickelson was born to Phil Mickelson Sr., and Mary Mickelson.

Phil Mickelson Siblings: Who are Tim Mickelson and Tina Mickelson?
