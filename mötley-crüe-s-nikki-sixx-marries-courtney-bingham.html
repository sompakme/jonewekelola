Musician Nikki Sixx has married Courtney Bingham in a ceremony at Greystone Estate in Beverly Hills, California, making him a groom for the third time.The Mötley Crüe bass guitarist said “I do” to the model on Saturday in front of 130 guests and couldn’t wait to share the new son Twitter, writing: “Hot damn. The bride looked beautiful in a gown from her aunt's Janene's Bridal Boutique while Nikki donned a black tuxedo suit with a bandana on his head. He has four children, Frankie-Jean with Donna, plus Decker Nilsson, Storm Brieanne and Gunner Nicholas with Brandi.Before his two-year relationship with current wife Courtney, Nikki was linked to Kat Von D, the tattoo artist who was dating Sandra Bullock’s ex-husband when his adultery was uncovered.Nikki popped the question to Courtney during a holiday in St Bart’s he revealed in November 2012. The star founded Mötley Crüe in 1980 with Tommy Lee who got engaged recently to his singer partner Sofi.
