Marcus Camby’s Basketball Career

Marcus Camby’s journey in the NBA began in 1996 when he was selected as the second overall pick by the Toronto Raptors. His defensive skills were evident from the start, earning him the NBA Defensive Player of the Year award in 2007. Throughout his career, Camby played for several teams including the New York Knicks, Denver Nuggets, Los Angeles Clippers, Portland Trail Blazers, and Houston Rockets.

Salary Earnings from NBA

During his tenure in the NBA, Camby earned significant income. His highest annual salary was $11.25 million during the 2010-2011 season with the Portland Trail Blazers. His total career earnings from NBA salaries alone are estimated to be over $130 million.
