Mark Wills’ Early Life

Mark Wills was born in Cleveland, Tennessee, and developed a passion for music at a young age. He began performing in local bars and clubs before moving to Atlanta, Georgia, to pursue his music career. His talent and dedication soon caught the attention of record labels.

Music Career

Wills signed his first record deal with Mercury Records in 1996. His debut album, “Mark Wills,” was a commercial success, producing three top 10 hits on the Billboard Country charts. His subsequent albums, “Wish You Were Here” and “Permanently,” also achieved significant success.
