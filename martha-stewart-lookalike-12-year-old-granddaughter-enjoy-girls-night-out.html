Martha Stewart, lookalike 12-year-old granddaughter enjoy girls night out

Martha, 81, is a grandma to two  

    Girls night! Martha Stewart was joined by look-alike 12-year-old granddaughter Jude on Thursday March 30 as the pair attended the opening of new Broadway play

    The 81-year-old chef, lifestule guru and businesswoman, is mom to Alexis - the former co-host of on the Hallmark Channel alongside co-host Jennifer Hutt - posed for pictures alongside Jude, 12, who is Martha's daughter Alexis's oldest child. Martha is also grandmother to Alexis's son Truman, 11.

    She welcomed both childsren via surrogacy.

    Posing for pictures with Jude, Martha wore her classic look of a button-down shirt under a dark sweater and black slacks, paired with black sneakers. Jude, meanwhile, rocked a sparkly 60s-inspired mini dress and knee-high black boots.

    The two may have over 70 yeas between them, but they looked so similar thanks to their bone structure and their beaming smiles.

    While she has multiple homes Martha lives primarily in a small hamlet outside of Bedford, Connecticut with various animals including peacocks. In May last year, Martha confirmed that she had "21 of these glorious birds," adding that they "do not smell," and are "so clean".

    But sadly in July 2022 Martha posted the heartbreaking news that six of her peacocks had been savagely killed by a marauding group of coyotes at her compound. "RIP beautiful BlueBoy," she wrote alongside a video of her magnificent peacock. 

    "The coyotes came in broad daylight and devoured him and five others including the magnificent White Boy."

    Among many celeb pals, Martha is close friends with Blake Lively and Ryan Renyolds, after the pair moved into her New York neighborhood in the early 2010s, and Martha helped the pair with their South Carolina wedding after it was "becoming a disaster", according to Blake.

    In 2022 Martha held a tag sale at her farm to sell her personal collection of furniture, tableware, art, linens, clothing, plants, decorations, and more items with all proceeds going to the Martha Stewart Centers for Living at the Mount Sinai Hospital. 

    Mom-of-four Blake attended and shared pictures on her Instagram rocking denim overalls over a white tank as she shopped.
