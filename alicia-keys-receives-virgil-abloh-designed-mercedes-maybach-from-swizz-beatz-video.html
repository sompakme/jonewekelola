Alicia Keys Receives Virgil Abloh-Designed Mercedes-Maybach From Swizz Beatz: Video

Alicia Keys previously celebrated her 42nd birthday back in January, but she's just now getting to enjoy one of the luxurious presents purchased for her by her doting husband, Swizz Beatz. On the 25th of the first month of the year, the renowned vocalist celebrated with some sunshine in a bikini, and her producer beau serenaded her, much to her pleasure. While the gift was certainly thoughtful and romantic, it has nothing on what just finally came through on Sunday (March 26).

In a video posted to her Instagram yesterday evening, the "Fallin'" hitmaker and her two sons unveiled a custom Mercedes-Maybach whip for Keys, which was designed by the late Virgil Abloh.
