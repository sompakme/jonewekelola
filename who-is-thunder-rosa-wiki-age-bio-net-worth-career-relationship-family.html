Who is Thunder Rosa? Wiki, Age, Bio, Net Worth, Career, Relationship, Family

  • Thunder Rosa is a renowned Mexican-born American professional wrestler and former mixed martial artist.
  • She is currently signed with All Elite Wrestling (AEW) and holds the AEW Women’s World Championship in her first reign.
  • Thunder Rosa gained recognition for her appearances in the TV series “Lucha Underground” as Kobra Moon, leader of the Reptile Tribe.

Husband and Children

  • Thunder Rosa is happily married to Bryan Cervantes since 2006, and they have a child named Anakin Cervantes.

Parents and Ethnicity

  • Details about Thunder Rosa’s parents and ethnicity are not available, but she is of Mexican-American nationality.

Wiki, Biography, and Age

  • Thunder Rosa, whose real name is Melissa Cervantes, was born on July 22, 1986, in Tijuana, Baja California, Mexico.
  • She was a bright student and attended a private high school in Tijuana. Later, she graduated with a Bachelor’s Degree in Sociology from the University of California, Berkeley.
  • Before her wrestling career, she worked with adults struggling with mental illness, homelessness, and substance abuse.

Physical Characteristics

  • Thunder Rosa stands at a height of 5 feet 3 inches and weighs 54 kg.
  • She has an impressive wrestling career, making her debut in 2014 and founding Mission Pro Wrestling (MPW), a women’s wrestling promotion company based in Texas.
  • Thunder Rosa also ventured into mixed martial arts in 2019 at Combate Americas.

Net Worth

  • With her successful wrestling career, Thunder Rosa has amassed an estimated net worth of $2.5 million.

Conclusion

  • Thunder Rosa has made a significant impact in the world of professional wrestling and mixed martial arts.
  • Her achievements and talent have earned her the AEW Women’s World Championship and a substantial net worth.
  • As she continues to excel in her career, fans eagerly await her future endeavors.
