How Many Chicago Tv Shows Are There

Windy City, Windy Dramas: A Deep Dive into the Neverending Chicago TV Universe

Ah, Chicago. City of broad shoulders, deep-dish pizza, and... an alarming number of TV shows set within its bustling streets. But fear not, fellow couch potato, for I, your intrepid guide to the world of television, am here to crack the code on Chicago's small-screen domination.

The Big Three (and a Half): The Pillars of Primetime

First things first, there's the granddaddy of them all: the Chicago franchise. This interconnected beast has been gracing our televisions since 2012, and it's a hydra with multiple heads:

  • Chicago Fire: Brace yourself for heart-stopping rescues and firefighter beefcake (don't judge, it's a public service).
  • Chicago P.D.: These guys are the undercover heroes (and occasional donut connoisseurs) keeping the Windy City safe from bad guys.
  • Chicago Med: Where the real drama unfolds. Not from gang wars, but from medical emergencies and the never-ending cycle of resident doctors falling in love.

Chicago Justice briefly joined the party, but met its demise after one season. Guess the legal system just couldn't compete with the allure of firetrucks and stethoscopes.

Fun Fact: All these shows like to share a bar called Molly's. It's basically the Cheers of Chicago TV, minus the witty banter (mostly).

Beyond the Franchise: A Rogues' Gallery of Windy City Dramas

But wait, there's more! Chicago's got a magnetic pull on TV producers, because there have been...well, a lot of other shows. Here's a taste:

  • Shameless: A dramedy about a dysfunctional Southside family that will make you laugh, cry, and question your own life choices.
  • Early Edition: Remember that show where the guy got tomorrow's newspaper today? Yeah, that was in Chicago.

Bonus: Even Batman couldn't resist Chicago's allure. Gotham wasn't exactly sunshine and rainbows, but hey, it gave us a young Jim Gordon cleaning up the gritty streets.

So, How Many Shows Does That Make?

Honestly, my friend, it's a moving target. New shows pop up, some fade away quicker than a Chicago deep dish disappears after a long day. But one thing's for sure: When it comes to drama and excitement, Chicago delivers on the small screen in a big way.

So grab your remote, some popcorn, and settle in for a marathon. There's a whole Windy City full of stories waiting to be explored.
