Taylor Swift's Grammy Awards 2016 Dress

It was all about the color blocking for Taylor Swift as she arrived at the 2016 Grammys red carpet at Staples Center in Los Angeles on Monday evening.

The 26-year-old, who is nominated for seven awards, shone in a two-piece orange and pink ensemble by Versace, which she styled with a brand-new bob and her signature cat eye. We’ll have to wait and see if she has any quick costume changes planned for her show-opener.

Much like her look last year, the “Style” singer made sure to show off those mile-long legs, too.
