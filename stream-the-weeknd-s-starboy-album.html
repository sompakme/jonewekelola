Stream The Weeknd's &quot;Starboy&quot; Album

Here it is. After months of promotion, which included four singles, SNL & AMA performances, & a short film called "M A N I A," The Weeknd's highly anticipated third studio album, Starboy, has officially arrived on iTunes.

Laced with 18 tracks in total, the follow up to last year’s Beauty Behind The Madness features guest appearances from Daft Punk twice, Kendrick Lamar (“Sidewalks”), Future (“All I Know”), and Lana Del Ray for a “Stargirl interlude”. Meanwhile, production is being handled by the likes of Cashmere Cat, Benny Blanco, Draft Punk, Jake One, Frank Dukes, Ben Billions, Mano, and more.

Having already heard singles “Starboy,” “False Alarm,” “Monster Party” & “I Feel It Coming” featuring Daft Punk, fans can now stream the entire album via Apple Music (below). Be sure to show your support on iTunes if you're feeling it.
