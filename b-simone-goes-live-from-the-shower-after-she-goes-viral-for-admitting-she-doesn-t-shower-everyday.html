B. Simone Goes Live From The Shower After She Goes Viral For Admitting She Doesn't Shower Everyday

B. Simone has set the internet on fire.

While on an episode of her podcast, B. Simone admits that she does not shower everyday- rather a few times a week. She explains in the clip that if her time is limited, she'd rather sleep than shower. She also says that if showers on Monday, the next time she'd shower is Wednesday or Thursday.

Of course, the clip of that podcast goes viral, and social media has mixed reactions regarding Simone's hygiene. One Twitter user wrote, "so i was listening to a B. Simone podcast and she don’t wash. Ion like that. Well she said, if i wash Monday I’m more than likely i am not going to wash until Thursday & that is just…." another one says, "B Simone has not one redeemable quality & I’ve learned this against my will..."

B. clapped back at the backlash by going on Instagram live while she's in the shower to prove that she's clean.
