Who is Ara Mina Dating Now? All About Her Relationships, Past and Present

Spread the love             

Ara Mina is a Filipino actress and singer who has been in the industry for over two decades. She has had her fair share of ups and downs in her personal life, particularly when it comes to her romantic relationships. In this article, we will take a closer look at who Ara Mina is dating now, as well as her past relationships, current relationship status, rumors, and some interesting facts.

Current Relationship Status:

As of this writing, Ara Mina is engaged to businessman Dave Almarinez. The couple got engaged in March 2021, after Dave proposed to Ara in a private island in Palawan. They have been dating for over a year before getting engaged. Ara has been very open about her relationship with Dave, sharing photos and sweet messages on social media.

Who is Dave Almarinez?

Dave Almarinez is a CEO of a construction company and a former politician. He ran for a senatorial seat in the Philippines in 2019 but was not elected. He is also a divorcee with two kids from his previous marriage.

Past Relationships:

Ara Mina’s romantic life has been the subject of media attention for years. She has been linked to several actors and personalities in the past, including Jomari Yllana, Polo Ravales, and Patrick Meneses. She was also in a high-profile relationship with Bulacan Mayor Patrick Meneses, with whom she has a daughter named Amanda Gabrielle.

Rumors:

There have been rumors that Ara Mina and Dave Almarinez’s engagement was rushed because of her pregnancy. However, Ara has denied these rumors and said that she is not pregnant. There were also rumors in the past that Ara Mina and Jomari Yllana had a secret wedding, but they were never confirmed.

Breakup:

Ara Mina has had her fair share of heartbreaks in the past. She was in a long-term relationship with Jomari Yllana in the early 2000s, but they eventually broke up. She also had a publicized breakup with Patrick Meneses in 2014.

Facts:

  • Ara Mina’s real name is Hazel Pascual Reyes.
  • She started her showbiz career in 1996, when she joined the cast of the teen-oriented show “Gimik.”
  • She has won several awards for her acting, including a Best Actress award for the movie “Mano Po 2” in 2003.
  • Aside from acting, Ara is also a successful businesswoman. She owns Hazelberry Cafe, a coffee shop in Quezon City.
  • Ara is also a doting mother to her daughter, Amanda Gabrielle, whom she had with Patrick Meneses.

Conclusion:

Ara Mina has had a colorful romantic history, but it seems like she has finally found her happily ever after with Dave Almarinez. We wish her all the best in her future endeavors, both in her personal and professional life.
