Melania Trump is Jackie Kennedy's doppelganger in matching coat

    Melania Trump's ensemble of a powder blue coat and long gloves for her husband Donald Trump's inauguration on Friday has earned comparisons to Jackie Kennedy's style, and this side-by-side photo of Melania with the former First Lady has revealed just how similar the two looks are.

    Jackie and Melania wore very similar coats

    A known style icon, Jackie Kennedy can be seen wearing a pink coat with a cowl neckline in the photo, which is very similar to the Ralph Lauren suit donned by Melania on Friday. Her decision to wear gloves completed the retro look which emulated Jackie's demure style. According to the Melania had previously named Jackie O as one of her style icons. Back in 1999 she was asked what her style would be should she ever become First Lady, and she replied: "I would be very traditional. Like Betty Ford or Jackie Kennedy." Her look was also compared to Jackie's own inauguration outfit back in 1961, as the wife of John F. Kennedy also wore a powder blue outfit.
