Introduction

Crawford is known for his swift boxing style, tactical approach, and powerful punches. His journey in the boxing world has not only earned him fame but also a significant amount of wealth. Bud Crawford’s Early Life and Career

Born and raised in Omaha, Nebraska, Crawford started boxing at the age of seven. His talent was evident from a young age, and he quickly rose through the ranks in the amateur boxing world. He turned professional in 2008 and has since then established himself as one of the best boxers in the world.

Major Achievements

Crawford’s major achievements include winning the WBO welterweight title in 2018, the undisputed light welterweight title in 2017, and the WBO lightweight title in 2014. He was also named Fighter of the Year by the Boxing Writers Association of America in 2014 and 2017.

Current Net Worth

Income from Boxing Matches

Endorsements and Sponsorships
