Donna Vekic Net Worth 2023, Salary, Endorsements, Cars, House and More

Donna Vekic is a well-known professional tennis player from Croatia who achieved a career-high singles ranking of No. 19 in November 2019. She has won four WTA singles titles, including the 2023 Monterrey Open, the 2021 Courmayeur Ladies Open and the 2017 Nottingham Open. The 27-year-old's best performance at a major came at the 2023 Australian Open, where she lost to Aryna Sabalenka in the quarterfinals.

Vekic is an aggressive baseline player who has a powerful first serve. She likes to dominate points from the baseline, especially from the forehand wing. Her preferred surface is hard and grass courts.

Donna Vekic's net worth

As per online sources, Donna Vekic's net worth is estimated at around $5 million as of 2023. Her primary income sources include tennis prize money, brand partnerships and her own home fragrance brand.

Donna Vekic Early Career

Vekic was born in Osijek, Croatia and started playing tennis when she was six. She won her first national 12s title when she was 10. After gaining success at the junior level, she decided to turn pro in September 2012.

Vekic reached her first WTA final at the Tashkent Open and became the youngest player in six years to reach the final. She made her Grand Slam debut at the 2013 Australian Open, defeating Andrea Hlavackova in the first round.

Donna Vekic Endorsements

As a successful player on the WTA circuit for more than a decade, Vekic has signed partnerships with multiple brands. Some of her endorsements are listed below:

Donna Sport

Vekic has joined hands with men's sports brand Uomo Sport to create her own women's clothing line named Donna Sport. The clothing line was launched in the first week of 2023.

Yonex

Vekic has been associated with tennis equipment manufacturer Yonex since her junior career. She currently uses the Yonex VCORE 100.

Rosal

Vekic signed a multi-year agreement with skincare cosmetic brand Rosal. She promotes company products on her social media channel.

F.P. Journe

Vekic is in an endorsement deal with Swiss French watchmaker F.P. Journe and is often seen wearing their watches.

Old Pilot

Vekic also used to endorse products designed by Old Pilot.

Donna Vekic Investments

Vekic is a successful businesswoman and an entrepreneur. She launched her own home fragrance brand named DNNA in 2021. DNNA is famous for selling scented candles and room sticks made of beeswax and reed diffusers. As per sources, a major portion of revenue is donated to bee conservation in her home country, Croatia.

Donna Vekic House

Vekic is a famous and wealthy personality in the tennis fraternity. She owns a villa in her home town of Osijek, where she lives with her parents. In 2019, she purchased an apartment on the 12th floor of a building in Monte Carlo as well. However, the price of this apartment is not available.
