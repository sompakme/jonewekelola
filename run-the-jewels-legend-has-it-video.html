Run The Jewels &quot;Legend Has It&quot; Video

Run The Jewels, otherwise known as the duo made up of Killer Mike and El-P, are known to get pretty adventurous with their videos, and the clip for RTJ3's "Legend Has It" is no exception. The clip is both visually exhilarating and politically-charged. Things begin in a police station, as Mike and El pop some acid and begin to multiply. From there, they join a police lineup where things become even more surreal. Eventually, we're given increasing mass incarceration statistics, just one of many elements of the video that pairs the playful chemistry of the group with deep social commentary.
