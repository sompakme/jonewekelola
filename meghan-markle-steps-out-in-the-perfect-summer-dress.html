Meghan Markle steps out in the perfect summer dress

Getty Images

As most Londoners are today, Meghan Markle looked to be embracing the first of the spring weather as she stepped out with Prince Harry for a reception at the Commonwealth Youth Forum. The future duchess opted to wear a very spring-appropriate pinstripe dress from Altuzarra.

The black-and-white dress – which is still available to buy here – features plenty of chunky buttons and a fabric belt cinching in the waist. Markle teamed it with a navy blazer (from Australian label Camilla and Marc), which she wore in a 'shrobing' fashion, black strappy court shoes and a cross-body bag from Australian accessories label Oroton (which costs just £171).
