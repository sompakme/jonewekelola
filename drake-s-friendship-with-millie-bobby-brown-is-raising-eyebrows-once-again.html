Drake's Friendship With Millie Bobby Brown Is Raising Eyebrows Once Again

On February 22, a few days after Millie Bobby Brown's 18th birthday, a TikTok went viral for poking fun at the actor and Drake's relationship. TikToker Skylar Sheerman posted a video that featured a photo of Brown with the text "Drake after Millie's 18th birthday announcement." She added audio from "SpongeBob SquarePants" that mocked Drake by saying, "Finally, I've been trying to catch you boys all day." The TikTok went viral, garnering a reaction from the "Knife Talk" rapper himself. In response, Drake seemed to take it in stride. "Loool," he wrote, per Page Six. 

Although Drake seemingly found the TikTok mocking his friendship with Brown humorous, some fans were still uneasy. "Ever sense [sic] the Millie Bobby Brown thing, something about Drake doesn't sit right with me, everything he does makes me cringe idk," one Twitter user wrote. "I just know Drake hit Millie Bobby Brown with an eight-paragraph birthday text with footnotes," another joked.

Meanwhile, Brown celebrated her birthday alongside her boyfriend Jake Bongiovi, the son of rock star Jon Bon Jovi. The "Stranger Things" star uploaded two snaps to Instagram on February 19, in which the pair wore Barbie and Ken-inspired outfits. A few days later, Brown continued her birthday celebration with another Instagram post of her posing solo in a sparkling dress.
